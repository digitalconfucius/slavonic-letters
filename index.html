Church Slavonic Letters for English Speakers


А

Pronounced as "a" in "father."

Б

Pronounced as "b" in "bed."

В

Pronounced as "v" in "victory."

Г

Pronounced as "g" in "go."

Д

Pronounced as "d" in "dog."

Ѳ

Fita, similar to "th" in "this"; often pronounced as "f."
